About Us

Swanky Miles is the new voice of Afro-fusion—a Lagos-born musical innovator blending Afrobeat, Afroswing, and palm wine rhythms with deeply personal storytelling. Born Adebayo Farouq Babatunde, he turns everyday realities into soundtracks for the soul, delivering emotionally rich, sonically vibrant music that transcends borders.

Whether he's reflecting on the fleeting nature of time in Life Is Short, celebrating joy in Stay Happy, or vibing to Lagos nightlife in Lagos Vibes, Swanky Miles invites listeners into his world—where rhythm meets resilience, and every beat tells a story. With a growing catalogue that pulses with energy, introspection, and cultural pride, Swanky is not just making music—he’s crafting a movement. For fans of conscious Afrobeat, genre-defiant melodies, and feel-good vibes, Swanky Miles is the name to watch.
